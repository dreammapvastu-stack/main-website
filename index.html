Living Room

According to Vastu, the main living room should be situated just south of the entrance verandah, leaving generous open space to the north and east for air and light circulation.

Master Bedroom

Bedrooms are placed south of the living area and towards the southwest corner so the heaviest elements of the home anchor the space, promoting rest and stability.

Pooja Room

The pooja (prayer) room is ideally located in the northeast corner. Idols may face east or west while the devotee faces west or east, with carved wooden paneling enhancing the sacred ambience.

Kitchen

The kitchen belongs in the southeast corner—the fire zone—to harmonize the elements and balance Akasha (space) with Agni (fire) for nourishment and health.
